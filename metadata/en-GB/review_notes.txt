To test the barcode and QR code scanning feature, please visit:
https://cards.serby.net/demo.html

This page contains:
1. A sample barcode (EAN-13) - scan to add a test loyalty card
2. A sample QR code - scan to add a test membership card

Testing steps:
1. Open the Cards app
2. Tap the + button to add a new card
3. Point the camera at either code on the demo page
4. The app will detect and save the card
